Capyboo is a cute, calm desk companion robot designed to sit on your table and keep you company.
With a small display, expressive animations, and gentle interactions, Capyboo brings a relaxing and friendly presence to your workspace.

Built as a fun blend of hardware + software, Capyboo focuses on personality, simplicity, and delightful user experience rather than complexity.

It has 4 modes -

1. **Animation Mode** - Displays expressive face animations with different moods (idle, happy, love, sad). Capyboo randomly plays various animation sequences to keep you company.

2. **Weather Mode** - Shows current weather information including city name, temperature, humidity, and weather description on the display.

3. **Game Mode** - Play a fun Dino game! Jump over obstacles by pressing the touch sensor. Perfect for quick breaks during work.

4. **Clock Mode** - Displays a simple clock showing the current time.
